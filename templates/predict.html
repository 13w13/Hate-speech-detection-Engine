Our Mission

The idea of this work is to develop a new application able to detect hate speech and offensive messages. For a given tweet/retweet, the application is able to classify the comment as a hate message, an offensive message or neither hate speech nor offensive.

Why this project?

The online hate speech messages is proliferating and especially on Twitter. Users can write what ever their want even under fake identities. The content of hateful tweets could be toward anything and anyone, and if the writor is famous on Tweeter it could have huge social consequences. This phenomenon could also target companies that have difficulties to control unfair hate message toward them.

For who?

This app can be use for any company who wants to prevent and control hateful messages from Twitter.

How to use it?

First, you have to scrap all tweets related to hastags or keywords you are interested in from the Twitter API . Then, you can upload on the dedicated field of our website the tweets csv. At the end, our hate speech analysor will give you back your csv file with an additional predictive column.
